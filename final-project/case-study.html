Case Study

New Perpectives Theatre Company

Objectives

From August to December of 2023, I had the opportunity to work with NY-basedNew Perspectives Theatre Company

I was tasked with developing their social channels to:

  • Better inform the NYcommunity of the organization's varied programming
  • Increase in-person visits and ticket sales
  • Make connections with other theatre organizations and professionals interested in working with the non-profit

Results

Between Q3 and Q4, I was able to:

  • Raise instagram traffic by 231.1%
  • Create 13,983 organic impressions
  • Increase overall site visits by 13%
